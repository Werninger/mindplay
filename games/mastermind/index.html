mindplay
How to play: Crack the secret 4-color code in up to 10 guesses.

After each guess you get feedback dots:
Filled (dark) = right color, right position.
Ring (outlined) = right color, wrong position.
No dot = that color isn't in the code.

Tap a color in the palette, then tap the slot you want to fill. Submit when all 4 slots are filled.
